About

Mehfil started in 2006, as a pocked friendly, family restaurant for the economic
working class of the city, maintaining quality at it's best, we've had the experience
of Grand Hotel, as our parent brand that has been serving since 1935.Mehfil has won
half a dozen award for the best biryani. It also has received the Best Biryani First Runner-up
Award from Vijay Deverkonda in 2017 at Times Biryani and Haleem Contest. Mehfil is Hyderabad
City Icon Award for Excellence in the category of Hyderabadi Cuisine for the year 2018.
